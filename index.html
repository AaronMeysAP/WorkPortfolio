'The Ubered Eaths' | an Uber Eats commercial

Suno, Pixabay
January 2025

A pretty unserious take on the food delivery service advertisements.

Context: This is a school assignment, where we were tasked to make a food delivery ad. But it got a bit out of hand, and I ended up pushing the boundaries a bit.

The briefing for this was pretty strict, so some of the scenes feel pushed in a bit in hindsight. (These scenes were demanded)

Almost everything was done by me, including but not limited to:

  • Storyboarding
  • Location scouting
  • Directing
  • Comping
  • Sound design
  • 3D comping
  • Color Grading

Reflection:

Looking back, this is one of my more fun, but less successful projects. I remember having a blast thinking and realising the ideas, but the project I took was larger than we thought.

Most of the rotoscoping and comping was done in a time crunch, and you can definitly see that.


But as the famous quote goes:

"Success is not final, Failure is not Fatal: At least we tried... or something." ~ Winston Churchill

I think still putting this video on my portfolio is important because it shows some of my pitfalls. But maybe most importantly what aspects I still need to learn.

Actors: Ilya Leue, Martin Deboeck | Director: Aaron Meys | Artesis Plantijn University College Antwerp

Sports Drink Commercial

November 2023

'SAP' commercial - get some punk in this drink!

This short motion graphics commercial was my very first motion assignment ever, in wich I learned to use after effects and explore the boundaries of motion graphics.

Some notable things to say about this video:

  • The assets, sush as the lemon, the sportsperson, the bottle, logo, etc etc... were given to us, and needed to all be included in the final video.
  • Nor sound Nor music were allowed to be in the final product.
  • The explosion is hand animated in illustrator.
  • This project took about two weeks.

Since it was the first project I made using After Effects, I (of course) made some clear visible mistakes. I believe it achieved its goal and still stands up well to my other work today.
